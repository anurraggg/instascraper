Recipe:
Motichoor Laddu
Preparation time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Serves: 4-5

Ingredients:
For the batter:
2 cups besan
2 cup water
2 cups sugar
1 cup water
2 drops orange food colour
Oil to fry
2 tbsp ghee
Pistachios to garnish

Method:
Make a batter of besan and water. 
Heat oil in a pan.
Fill batter in a piping bag. pour over medium hot oil.
Make small moti.
Make a 1 string sugar syrup with the sugar and water. Add  the food colour and mix. Soak the boondis in the syrup. Add the desi and mix well. Shape the laddus and garnish with pistachios and serve.
