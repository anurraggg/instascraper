Ema Datsi Yipee

Ingredients:
1 pack yipee noodles
3 green chilies
3 garlic cloves
2 small onions
1 capsicum
Yipee tastemaker
3 Cheese slices
Instructions:
1. Boil green chilies , garlic onions &,capsicum in 1 cup water
2. Add in cheese slices cook that till it dissolves and reduces to half
3. Add in the Yippee masala sachet abd yippee noodles
4. Serve hot!
