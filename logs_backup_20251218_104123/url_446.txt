Method
 1. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until smooth.
 2. Add jaggery and mix well until combined.
 3. Pour in milk little by little and stir.
 4. Sift in the wheat flour and fold gently into the mixture.
 5. Add cardamom powder, a pinch of salt, and vanilla extract if using. Mix into a smooth, thick batter.
 6. Heat an appe pan and lightly grease with ghee.
 7. Pour spoonfuls of batter into each cavity, cover, and cook on low flame for about 5 minutes.
 8. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown and cooked through.
 9. Serve warm as a snack or sweet treat.
